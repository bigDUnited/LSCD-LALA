Use Case: Reserve a ticket
Primary Actor: Registered Customer
Scope: a ticket reservation system
Level: !
Brief: Customer reserves a ticket on the system for himself.
Postconditions
    Minimal Guarantees:
    Success Guarantees:
        A reservation is saved on the system.
	A reservation is shown on the reservation overview page.
Preconditions: Customer has to be logged in.
Triggers: The customer creates a ticket reservation entry in the database.
Basic flow:
      Customer logs in
      Customer clicks "Add reservation" on overview page.
      Customer inputs date and time in a calendar form.
      Customer inputs number of people to be on the ferry.
      Customer selects "Reserve"
      A ticket reservation is shown on a overview page
      Customer logs out


Use Case: Making reservation for vechicles
Primary Actor: Registered Customer
Scope: a ticket reservation system
Level: !
Brief: Customer 
Postconditions
    Minimal Guarantees:
    Success Guarantees:
        A reservation including vechicles is saved on the system.
	A reservation including vechicles is shown on the reservation overview page.
Preconditions: 
    Customer has to be logged in.
    Customer has to be on the reservation page, placing a reservation.
Triggers: The customer creates a ticket reservation entry in the database.
Basic flow:
      Customer selects the optional "Add transport"(can be selected multiple times).
      Customer enters details about transport(class/type and registration number).
      Customer adds more transports or selects "Reserve" and finalizes reservation.
      Customer logs out
